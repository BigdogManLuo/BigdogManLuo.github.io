Online Aggregated Wind–Solar Probabilistic Forecasting and Trading in GB Electricity Market

📅 2023.12 – 2024.12
We proposed a comprehensive solution for the IEEE Hybrid Energy Forecasting and Trading Competition.
Our methods achieve accurate probabilistic forecasts for a wind–solar hybrid system and substantial trading revenue in the day-ahead electricity market.

Key contributions:

  1. Stacking-based ensemble of multi-source NWP forecasts for wind power
  2. Online post-processing for solar capacity shifts
  3. Probabilistic aggregation for hybrid quantile forecasts
  4. Stochastic trading strategy under uncertain prices

Keywords: wind forecasting · solar forecasting · probabilistic aggregation · trading strategy
